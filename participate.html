How to participate and contribute

This is one of our main goals to build and support the community around veter-project. If you want to contribute, discuss something, share with community or just keep yourself informed about latest development of our project, please consider the following ways to participate:
  • Use veter-project mail list to discuss related topics
  • Join Veterobot Google community as a simple way to share some information and read latest news.
  • Follow @veterobot on Twitter to receive most up-to-date news about our project
  • If you are software or hardware developer or 3D designer (or just curious) you might be interested to clone relevant git repositories, make your changes and send pull request to include your improvements back to the main-line code. You might be also interested to read the documentation for developers.
